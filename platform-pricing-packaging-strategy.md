# B2B Platform Pricing, Packaging, and Monetization Models 💰

An effective product strategy requires a robust commercial framework. This document outlines the value-based packaging structure engineered to monetize our platform engine across diverse enterprise business segments.

---

## 📦 The Three-Tier Packaging Topology

To capture maximum consumer surplus across early-stage developers and global holding companies, the product features are packaged into three distinct tiers based on infrastructure usage thresholds and operational SLAs.

### 1. Core Tier (Developer Self-Service)
* **Target Audience:** Individual engineers and small dev cells looking to validate cloud-native side-by-side concepts.
* **Pricing Model:** Flat-rate baseline platform subscription ($0 / Month) with tight consumption caps.
* **Usage Threshold Boundaries:** Up to 1,000 automated API key executions per month; standard community forum support channels.

### 2. Growth Tier (Scale Business)
* **Target Audience:** Expanding localized enterprises consolidating mid-sized legacy middleware frameworks.
* **Pricing Model:** Hybrid baseline subscription ($499 / Month) + metered usage overages ($0.05 per individual credential generation loop).
* **Usage Threshold Boundaries:** Up to 50,000 monthly transactions; guaranteed 99.9% platform availability uptime SLA.

### 3. Enterprise Tier (Global Multi-Cloud)
* **Target Audience:** Fortune 500 organizations running highly distributed, multi-region cloud infrastructures.
* **Pricing Model:** Pure value-based enterprise agreement commitments tailored to total connected infrastructure footprints.
* **Usage Threshold Boundaries:** Unlimited token executions; cross-hyperscaler Disaster Recovery failover routing; dedicated technical success managers; 99.99% operational uptime SLA.
